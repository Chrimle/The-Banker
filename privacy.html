1. Overview

This website (The Banker) uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site and improve its content. Google Analytics collects anonymous information such as pages visited, time spent on the site, and your browser/device type.

2. What data is collected

  • Pages you view and actions you take

  • Browser, operating system, and device information

Your full IP address is anonymized before being stored by Google.

3. Who processes the data

This site uses Google Analytics provided by Google LLC, which may store and process the information on servers in the United States and other countries.

4. How to opt out

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by:

  • Rejecting cookies in the consent banner shown on your first visit
  • Installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Disabling JavaScript in your browser (may affect site functionality)

5. Cookies

Google Analytics uses cookies to distinguish between unique visitors. These cookies do not contain personally identifiable information. If you are in the European Union, the United Kingdom, or other regions with similar laws, you can reject these cookies via the consent banner shown on your first visit.
