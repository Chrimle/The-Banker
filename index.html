How to play

Welcome to The Banker!

Here are some tips to get you started...

Controls

  • Click & Hold - to pick up a bill.
  • Double-click - to flip a bill.
  • Mouse Wheel - to rotate a bill.

Customer Requests

  • Deposits
    1. Empty the Transfer Box.
    2. Close the Transfer Box.
    3. The customer will place the deposit.
    4. Open the Transfer Box.
    5. Empty the Transfer Box.
    6. Close the Transfer Box.
      7. The customer will complain if the deposit box is not emptied!

  • Withdraws
    1. Place bills totalling the requested sum into the Transfer Box.
    2. Close the Transfer Box.
      3. The customer will complain if the given sum is not equal to the requested sum!

    TIP: Giving the customer the fewest bills possible will be counted as a Perfect Withdraw!
