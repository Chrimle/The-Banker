The Banker Game

After a total societal crash caused by AI, trust in technology is non-existent. Electronics have been abandoned and mostly outlawed, leaving people to rely on analog methods for communication, work, everything else. Communities are tight-knit and local, with trades, agreements, and daily life conducted face-to-face. Improvised human-operated mechanical devices are revitalizing past technologies - which are under heavy scrutiny by the various government bodies, conducting inspections for certifications and legal violations. Life is slower, and deliberate, as society struggles to rebuild a world where every action must be verified and nothing can be taken on faith in a system once dominated by Artificial Intelligence.
Since The Disconnect, You have been assigned your first job. As a bank teller at the first regional bank, you will play a crucial role in building trust in a new banking system and aid the community financially. Do this well, and all parties will benefit. But if you do not, you will be made an example, that is for sure...
